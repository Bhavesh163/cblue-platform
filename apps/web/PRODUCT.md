# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CBLUE serves customers booking household fixers, project teams, and professionals; verified partners receiving and managing work; and administrators reviewing credentials, fraud risk, operations, and connected BLUE workflows. Users work in Thai, English, or Chinese across desktop and mobile, often while coordinating live service requests, meetings, chat, and job completion.

## Product Purpose

CBLUE provides trustworthy service matching and an auditable 11-step fixer and professional workflow. Success means each eligible customer request reaches correctly matched local partners, the selected scope and budget remain identical through PO acceptance, and both parties see the same current workflow state across requests, active jobs, meetings, chat, alerts, rating, and history.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, clear, and efficient. CBLUE should feel calm and operationally dependable, with direct language, familiar controls, and visible state rather than decorative complexity.

## Anti-references

CBLUE must not resemble a demo dashboard, a marketing-heavy workflow shell, or a system that invents data to fill empty states. Avoid mock providers, static jobs, browser-only workflow truth, duplicated cards, transient disappearance, ambiguous actions, decorative motion, and inconsistent status labels across customer and partner views.

## Design Principles

1. Persisted state is authoritative. Every workflow surface projects the same backend order, status history, budget breakdown, attachments, chat, and review state.
2. Make the next action unmistakable. Requests show only actions currently required from that user; active jobs show live work; terminal work appears only in history.
3. Explain consequential decisions. Matching, tier evaluation, fees, budget lines, and exclusions must be traceable without allowing AI to invent facts.
4. Preserve cross-device continuity. Refreshes, token renewal, navigation, and another browser must not erase or advance workflow state.
5. Prefer honest empty, loading, and error states over placeholders or fabricated content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA across Thai, English, and Chinese. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, semantic controls, screen-reader labels, reduced motion, sufficient contrast, responsive text, and touch targets of at least 44 by 44 CSS pixels where practical.
